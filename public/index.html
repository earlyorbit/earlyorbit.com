identifying startup vectors for a digital native world

Early Orbit's core team is Bjørn Stabell and Michael Welch, who together founded the startup studio Exoweb Labs, where they dreamt up and launched the mobile gaming company Happylatte ($8.5M raised from Index Ventures) whose games reached top download and grossing spots and app store market intelligence leader, App Annie ($157M raised from IDG, Sequoia, IVP, Greycroft, Infinity).

Identifying startup opportunities requires keeping the pulse of technological and societal change and keeping human needs first and foremost. Changes map to vectors that help identify future patterns, and these patterns help identify opportunities. We believe there are currently several opportunities for breaking old habits and rethinking tools for a native digital world.
